ABOUT

Emergent Beacon is a small video series trying to ask big questions. Through research, critical thought, and effective communication, hopefully you will walk away from it with all the tools you need to ask questions too.

Emergent Beacon was developed in late 2020 and the first video posted in January 2021. The show is known for its rich world, quality presentation, transparent research, custom music, and accessibility features like high-quality closed captions.

This kind of work takes a lot of effort and resources, so it is only possible with viewer support. If the show speaks to you in some way, please consider pledging to the patreon campaign so I can make more of it.
