ABOUT

Emergent Beacon is a video series on Youtube that teaches you how to confront big questions. Through research, critical thinking, and clear communication, Emergent Beacon models asking and answering questions so you can have all the tools you need to do the same.

The show is known for its rich world, quality presentation, transparent research, custom music, and accessibility features like high-quality closed captions.

This kind of work takes a lot of effort and resources, so it is only possible with viewer support. If the show speaks to you in some way, please consider pledging to the patreon campaign so I can make more of it.
